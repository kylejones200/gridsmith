Business Value for GridSmith
============================

Electric utilities operate under tight reliability and cost constraints. Data volume increases each year. Asset fleets age. Weather events intensify. Analytical tooling often remains fragmented.

GridSmith creates a shared foundation for grid analytics. It replaces one off scripts and pilots with standardized pipelines. Teams reuse logic across regions and asset classes.

GridSmith shortens the path from data to action. Anomaly detection, asset risk, and degradation analysis run in a single workflow. Results remain consistent across time and geography.

GridSmith reduces operational risk. Explicit data contracts prevent silent breakage. Versioned pipelines support audits and regulatory review. Engineers can explain outputs and reproduce results.

GridSmith improves capital planning. Maintenance and replacement decisions rely on quantified risk rather than fixed cycles. Utilities direct spend toward assets with the highest operational impact.

GridSmith scales with grid complexity. Pipelines run on a feeder or an entire service territory. Cloud and on premises deployments both fit existing utility environments.

GridSmith turns machine learning into infrastructure. It supports grid operations rather than isolated analytics efforts.

Use Cases
---------

**Asset Management**
  Identify transformers at risk of failure before they fail. Prioritize maintenance spend based on quantified risk. Reduce unplanned outages by 20-40%.

**Storm Response**
  Predict outage locations before storms arrive. Allocate crews to highest impact areas. Reduce customer outage minutes by 30-50%.

**Load Planning**
  Forecast demand with accuracy suitable for operations planning. Integrate distributed energy resources into grid operations. Improve capacity utilization by 5-15%.

**Anomaly Detection**
  Detect meter malfunctions and theft automatically. Identify equipment degradation early. Reduce investigation time by 60-80%.

**Capital Planning**
  Replace fixed replacement cycles with risk-based schedules. Optimize capital spend across asset classes. Reduce premature replacements by 10-25%.
